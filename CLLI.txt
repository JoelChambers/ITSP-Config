C - City (eg. WHTH for Whitehorse)
P - Provice (eg. YT for Yukon)
S - Site (eg. CM for Canyon Mountain)
E - Equipment (see below)

Equipment naming:

ABC

AB - equipment type
C  - equipment number

ES - ethernet switch
TS - TDM switch
TG - TDM gateway
IR - IP router
ST - SS7 transfer point (STP)
SC - SS7 control point (SCP)
SS - SS7 switching point (SSP)
